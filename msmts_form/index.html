Pre-reqs!

Three things are necessary to complete this form: your suit measurements, dress shirt measurements, and an email address that reports can be sent reports to. That's it.

Measurements for sportcoats, sweaters, pants, casual shirts, whatever, will be extrapolated from suit and dress shirt measurements that you provide.

You will have the opportunity to review and fine tune those extrapolations, but they're generated for your convenience.
